Frequently Asked Questions

Does this work with cart drawers?+

This app block is designed for your theme's main Cart page and is not compatible with cart drawers or pop-up notification carts.

Do I need to know how to code?+

Not at all! The button can be fully configured without any code. The "Custom CSS" feature is an optional tool for advanced users.

How is billing handled?+

All billing is handled securely and directly through your Shopify account. You can manage your subscription at any time.

Do you take feature requests?+

Absolutely! We are always looking to improve. If you have an idea, please reach out via email.

How can I contact support?+

You can reach our support team directly by emailing contact@b-moat.com.

Is this app actively maintained?+

Yes. CartConnect is actively maintained and updated to ensure compatibility with the latest Shopify features.

How long does it take for you to reply?+

Our goal is to respond within 4 business hours on weekdays and within 24 hours on weekends.

Will this app slow down my store?+

Not at all. The app loads asynchronously and is optimized to have a negligible impact on speed.

Do I need a WhatsApp Business account?+

No, regular WhatsApp works just fine. There are no extra charges beyond the app subscription.

Privacy Policy for CartConnect

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you install or use the CartConnect app (the "App") in connection with your Shopify-powered store.

Personal Information the App Collects

When you install the App, we are able to access certain information from your Shopify account. We collect the following types of personal information from you as a merchant:

  • Shop Domain: The domain of your Shopify store.
  • Shop name and Email.
  • Subscription Status: Your current subscription status for the App.

We collect personal information directly from you (e.g., through your Shopify account when you install the App).

How Do We Use Your Personal Information?

We use the personal information we collect from you to:

  • Provide and operate the App and its services.
  • Communicate with you regarding the App.
  • Manage your subscription to the App.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Sharing Your Personal Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. We may share your Personal Information to comply with applicable laws and regulations, to respond to a subpoena, search warrant, or other lawful request for information we receive, or to otherwise protect our rights.

Data Minimization

We are committed to the principle of data minimization and collect only the data necessary for the App to function (shop domain, subscription status).

Your Rights

If you are a European resident, you have the right to access personal information we hold about you and to ask that your personal information be corrected, updated, or deleted. If you would like to exercise this right, please contact us through the contact information below. We have implemented webhook endpoints for customers/data_request, customers/redact, and shop/redact to facilitate your data subject rights as required by GDPR.

Data Retention

When you uninstall the App, we delete all your information from our records.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time in order to reflect, for example, changes to our practices or for other operational, legal or regulatory reasons.

Contact Us

For more information about our privacy practices, if you have questions, or if you would like to make a complaint, please contact us by email at contact@b-moat.com.
